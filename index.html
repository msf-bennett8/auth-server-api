Kenyatta University Cycling Club

Welcome to the official page of the Kenyatta University Cycling Club. Join us for thrilling cycling adventures, training sessions, and community events!


The Club is dedicated to promoting the sport of cycling while fostering a sense of camaraderie among cyclists. The club is committed to inclusivity and diversity, welcoming cyclists of all skill levels, backgrounds, and identities. Safety is a top priority, with strict adherence to safety guidelines and risk management practices. The club actively engages with the Kenyatta University community and the broader cycling community through events and collaborations. It also emphasizes leadership, accountability, and professional development, providing networking opportunities and skill-building activities. Social events and regular rides are organized to enhance community spirit and enjoyment, while competitive races offer members the chance to test their skills and represent the club. Financial management is handled with transparency, ensuring that funds are used to support various club activities and initiatives.

Why Join Our Club?

  • Build endurance and strength
  • Compete in national and international events
  • Access to expert coaching and guidance
  • Be part of an active and supportive community
